Project

Emergency Vehicle Traffic Assistance System

AIM

The proposed model will notify the traffic light system or authorities as an advance alert, so chances of clearance of road before arrival of emergency vehicles is increased.It reduces the time complexity and helps to supply faster transportation services for the Emergency Vehicles.





Working

• At a junction, place CCTV Camera before the traffic signal at an approx. Distance of 50- 100m.
• The camera will scan the ongoing vehicles, when the Emergency vehicle is detected,the camera will capture the image and send the image to the Recording Device.The Device compares the image with the image present in its Database.If the images are matched then This will turn that specific lane into Green automatically, until the vehicle has crossed the junction.
• All the above process can me implemented with the help of Machine Learning.
• This will help the Vehicle to cross the junction as much as early possible.
